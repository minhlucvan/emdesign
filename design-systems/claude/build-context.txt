## Spacing
Base 4px. Stops: 4/8/12/16/24/32/48/96 (TW p-1/2/3/4/6/8/12, py-24).
Cards: feature/pricing p-8; code/connector p-6; CTA p-12.

## Radius & Depth
Radius: xs=4, sm=6, md=8(def), lg=12, xl=16, pill/full=9999.
Depth: color-block first. Flat/hairline/raised cream/dark surface/shadow (0 1px 3px rgba(20,20,19,0.08)). Focus: 3px coral 15%.

## Rules (top 3)
1. NO raw hex: bg-accent/text-text/border-border
2. NO hardcoded spacing: p-6 not p-[24px]
3. NO off-token colors: cream+coral+dark navy only
