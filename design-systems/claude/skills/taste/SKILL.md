# claude Taste Profile

**Brand fingerprint:** A warm-cream editorial interface defined by its deliberate counter-position against the cool-blue AI industry standard -- slab-serif display headlines and coral voltage accents give Claude the literary authority of a long-form publication, not a SaaS marketing page.

**Visual characteristics:** Three-tier surface hierarchy (cream canvas, cream feature cards, dark navy product mockups) creates rhythmic page pacing via alternating bands; the Copernicus slab-serif display (weight 400, negative tracking) paired with StyreneB humanist sans body delivers an editorial typographic voice that distinguishes Claude from every other AI tool.

**Anti-patterns:** Never use cool gray or pure white backgrounds (instantly loses the cream brand signal), bold serif display weights (reads as shouting), or sans-serif display headlines (makes the site feel like generic AI SaaS); avoid coral overuse on every element as it dilutes brand voltage into noise rather than emphasis.
